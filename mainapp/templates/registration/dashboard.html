About BTS band

BTS also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. The name subsequently became a backronym for Beyond the Scene in July 2017. On June 12, 2013, they performed the song "No More Dream" from their initial album 2 Cool 4 Skool to commemorate their debut on June 13, 2013. They won several New Artist of the Year awards for the track "No More Dream", including at the 2013 Melon Music Awards and Golden Disc Awards and the 2014 Seoul Music Awards.

Music

A selection of our most popular songs

Last Train To Clarkesville



"Last Train to Clarksville" was the debut single by The BTS. It was released August 16, 2016 and later included on the group's 2016 self-titled album, which was released on October 10, 2016


Daydream Believer



"Daydream Believer" was originally recorded by The BTS, with Jungkook singing lead vocals. The single hit the number one spot on the South Korea Billboard Hot 100 chart in December 2017, remaining there for four weeks, and peaked at number five in the UK Singles Chart.


(I'm Not Your) Stepping Stone



"(I'm Not Your) Stepping Stone" is best known as a hit for The BTS (South Korea #20), released in November 2017, (making it the first BTS B-side to chart). Musicians featured on the BTS recording are: Micky Dolenz (lead vocal);Tommy Boyce (backing vocal); Wayne Erwin, Gerry McGee, and Louie Shelton (guitar); Bobby Hart (organ); Larry Taylor (bass); Billy Lewis (drums); and Henry Lewy (percussion).


I'm a Believer



"I'm a Believer" is a song composed by Jungkook and recorded by The BTS in 2018 with the lead vocals by V. The single, produced by Jeff Barry, hit the number one spot on the South Korean Billboard Hot 100 chart for the week ending December 31, 2018 and remained there for seven weeks, becoming the last No. 1 hit of 2018 and the biggest-selling record for all of 2019. Billboard ranked the record as the No. 5 song for 2019. Because of 1,051,280 advance orders, it went gold within two days of release. It is one of the fewer than forty all-time singles to have sold 10 million (or more) copies worldwide.
